# Infrastructure Project Procurement Typology

## Sector Overview
Infrastructure projects include roads, highways, bridges, rail (heavy and light), ports, airports, and tunnels. Procurement involves bulk materials, specialized civil equipment, geotechnical works, and long-duration construction services.

## Common PO Types (Purchase Orders for Goods)

| PO Category | Typical Goods | Key Specifications | Lead Times |
|-------------|---------------|-------------------|------------|
| Asphalt and Bitumen | Hot mix asphalt, polymer-modified bitumen | SANS/EN 13108, SABS 1200 | 2-6 weeks |
| Concrete and Precast | Ready-mix, precast elements, pipes, culverts | SANS 10100, SANS 679, structural classes | 2-8 weeks |
| Reinforcement Steel | Rebar, mesh, prestressing strands | SANS 920, SANS 1566, grade 500W | 4-12 weeks |
| Structural Steel | Bridge girders, gantries, canopies | SANS 10162, hot-dip galvanized | 12-24 weeks |
| Geotextiles and Geosynthetics | Geogrids, geocells, geotextiles, geomembranes | GRI specifications, filtration requirements | 4-8 weeks |
| Electrical Systems | Lighting, signaling, SCADA, comms | SANS 10142, SABS IEC standards | 16-26 weeks |
| Drainage Systems | Pipes, culverts, manholes, catchpits | Concrete, HDPE, PVC, SANS 677 | 4-12 weeks |

## Common SO Tracking Requirements

| Category | SO Focus Areas | Critical SO Verification Points |
|----------|---------------|--------------------------------|
| Bulk Materials | Delivery schedules, stockpiling | Material test certificates, batch numbers |
| Precast Elements | Manufacturing schedule, curing | Strength test results, dimensional tolerances |
| Specialty Equipment | Manufacturing, shipping | FAT, installation manuals, warranty terms |
| Geotextiles | Roll dimensions, UV protection | Tensile test certificates, UV stability |

## Common WO Types (Work Orders for Services)

| WO Category | Typical Services | Compliance Requirements |
|-------------|-----------------|------------------------|
| Earthworks | Cut/fill, bulk earthworks, embankments | Compaction testing, FDT results, SANS 10100 |
| Pavement Construction | Subbase, base, wearing course | SABS 1200 DB/DM/SA tolerances |
| Bridge Construction | Piles, substructure, superstructure, bearings | SANS structural codes, bearing installation specs |
| Rail Construction | Track laying, signaling, electrification | SAR rail specifications, safety signaling |
| Tunneling | TBM operations, rock support, waterproofing | Ground monitoring, face stability, safety systems |
| Traffic Management | Diversions, temporary works, traffic light installation | Traffic management plans, safety standards |

## Key Goods vs Services Distinctions for Infrastructure

### Goods (PO Items)
- Bulk materials (asphalt, concrete, steel) with material testing requirements
- Precast elements with manufacturing quality assurance
- Geotextiles/geosynthetics with specification and certification requirements
- Traffic management equipment with signaling and communications integration

### Services (WO/Service Order Items)
- Earthworks with compaction and quality control testing requirements
- Bridge construction with structural inspection and testing phases
- Rail and airport works with specialized track and signaling compliance
- Tunneling with ground monitoring and safety systems

## Project-Specific Procurement Considerations
- **Roads/Highways:** Asphalt plants, paving equipment, traffic signage, earthworks
- **Bridges:** Piling equipment, formwork systems, lifting and placing equipment
- **Rail:** Track materials, signaling systems, electrification, rolling stock
- **Ports:** Dredging services, quay walls, marine equipment, navigation aids
- **Airports:** Pavement systems, runway lighting, navigation systems, terminal fitout
- **Tunnels:** TBM services, ground support, waterproofing systems, ventilation

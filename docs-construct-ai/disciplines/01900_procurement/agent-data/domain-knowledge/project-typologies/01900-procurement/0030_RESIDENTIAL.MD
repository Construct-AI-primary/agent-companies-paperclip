# Residential Buildings Project Procurement Typology

## Sector Overview

Residential projects encompass single-family homes, multi-unit developments (townhouses, apartments), high-rise residential towers, and luxury estates. Procurement involves building materials, finishing products, MEP (mechanical, electrical, plumbing) fit-out, and specialized trade services. Residential procurement is characterized by high-volume repeat items, aesthetic finish requirements, and strict adherence to building codes and occupancy timelines.

### Sub-Sectors

| Sub-Sector | Description | Examples |
|-----------|-------------|----------|
| **Single-Family** | Detached homes, villas, custom residences | Standalone houses, duplexes, cluster homes |
| **Multi-Unit** | Medium-density attached dwellings | Townhouses, walk-up apartments, condominiums |
| **High-Rise** | Tall residential towers (10+ storeys) | Apartment towers, mixed-use residential podiums |
| **Estates** | Large-scale luxury or gated communities | Master-planned communities, golf estates, resort residences |

## Key Goods vs Services Distinctions for Residential

### Goods (PO Items)
- Building materials with SANS compliance and batch consistency requirements
- Finishing materials with color matching and aesthetic approval requirements
- MEP equipment with SANS certification and warranty requirements
- Kitchen and joinery with sample approval and hardware specifications
- Lifts with long lead times and FAT requirements

### Services (WO/Service Order Items)
- Structural works with concrete testing and inspection hold points
- MEP installation requiring Certificate of Compliance (COC) on completion
- Finishing trades with workmanship standards and defect liability periods
- Lift installation with safety testing and commissioning certification

## Project-Specific Procurement Considerations

- **Single-Family Homes:** Standardized material packages, bulk purchasing for developments, appliance packages
- **Multi-Unit Developments:** Consistent finishing across units, bulk material procurement, phased delivery schedules
- **High-Rise Towers:** Long-lead lift procurement, concrete pump logistics, tower crane coordination, MEP riser coordination
- **Estates/Luxury:** Premium finish materials, custom joinery, imported fixtures, landscape procurement, security systems
- **Affordable Housing:** Cost-optimized material selection, standardized designs, volume discounts, local sourcing
- **Renovations/Refurbishments:** Material matching, existing structure compatibility, dust and noise mitigation, tenant management

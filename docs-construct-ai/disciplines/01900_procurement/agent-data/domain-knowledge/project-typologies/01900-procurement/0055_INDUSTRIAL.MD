# Industrial Facilities Project Procurement Typology

## Sector Overview

Industrial projects encompass manufacturing plants, warehouses and distribution centers, data centers, food processing facilities, and light industrial buildings. Procurement involves process equipment, material handling systems, specialized MEP, and industrial building systems. Industrial procurement is characterized by process-driven specifications, equipment performance guarantees, strict hygiene or cleanliness standards, and integration with production systems.

### Sub-Sectors

| Sub-Sector | Description | Examples |
|-----------|-------------|----------|
| **Manufacturing** | Production facilities for goods and products | Automotive plants, assembly lines, fabrication shops, pharmaceutical manufacturing |
| **Warehousing & Distribution** | Storage and logistics facilities | Fulfillment centers, cold storage, cross-dock facilities, bulk storage |
| **Data Centers** | Mission-critical computing facilities | Hyperscale data centers, colocation facilities, edge data centers |
| **Food Processing** | Food and beverage production facilities | Abattoirs, dairy processing, beverage bottling, packaged food plants |
| **Light Industrial** | General industrial and workshop facilities | Industrial parks, business parks, service centers, workshops |

## Key Goods vs Services Distinctions for Industrial

### Goods (PO Items)
- Process equipment with performance guarantees and FAT requirements
- Material handling systems with automation integration and safety certification
- HVAC and clean room equipment with ISO classification and HEPA certification
- Refrigeration systems with refrigerant compliance and temperature guarantees
- Structural steel with crane load ratings and fire protection specifications
- Electrical systems with UPS autonomy and generator sizing requirements

### Services (WO/Service Order Items)
- Process equipment installation with OEM procedures and alignment tolerances
- Material handling installation with load testing and automation integration
- Clean room construction with ISO 14644 certification and particle count testing
- Cold room construction with temperature verification and insulation testing
- Commissioning with performance testing against process guarantees
- Data center fit-out with tier certification and cooling verification

## Project-Specific Procurement Considerations

- **Manufacturing Plants:** Process equipment long lead times, production line integration, raw material handling, waste treatment
- **Warehouses/Distribution:** Racking systems, dock equipment, WMS integration, fleet management, lighting design
- **Data Centers:** Tier classification (I-IV), cooling redundancy, power distribution architecture, security integration, commissioning rigor
- **Food Processing:** Food-grade materials, hygiene standards, CIP (clean-in-place) systems, temperature control, regulatory compliance
- **Cold Storage:** Refrigeration system design, insulated panel procurement, temperature monitoring, backup systems
- **Pharmaceutical:** GMP compliance, clean room classification, validation documentation, material segregation
- **Automotive:** Production line integration, robotic systems, paint shop equipment, JIT delivery coordination

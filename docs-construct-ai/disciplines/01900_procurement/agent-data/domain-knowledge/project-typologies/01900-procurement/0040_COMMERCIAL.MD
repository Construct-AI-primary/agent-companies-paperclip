# Commercial Buildings Project Procurement Typology

## Sector Overview

Commercial projects encompass office buildings, retail centers, hospitality venues (hotels, restaurants), and mixed commercial developments. Procurement involves office fit-out, retail fixtures, MEP systems, building finishes, and specialized commercial equipment. Commercial procurement is characterized by fast-track schedules, tenant-specific fit-out requirements, brand-standard compliance, and high-quality aesthetic finishes.

### Sub-Sectors

| Sub-Sector | Description | Examples |
|-----------|-------------|----------|
| **Office** | Corporate headquarters, business parks, co-working spaces | Grade A offices, commercial towers, business centers |
| **Retail** | Shopping centers, strip malls, showrooms, supermarkets | Regional malls, retail parks, convenience centers |
| **Hospitality** | Hotels, resorts, restaurants, conference centers | Boutique hotels, chain hotels, convention centers |
| **Mixed Commercial** | Combined commercial uses, entertainment venues | Cinemas, gyms, medical centers, car dealerships |

## Key Goods vs Services Distinctions for Commercial

### Goods (PO Items)
- Curtain wall and glazing with thermal and acoustic performance requirements
- Office fit-out components with modular specifications and fire ratings
- Retail fixtures with brand standard compliance and display integration
- Hospitality FF&E with global sourcing, fire-rated fabrics, and brand alignment
- MEP equipment with energy efficiency ratings and BMS integration
- Lifts with destination control systems and long lead times

### Services (WO/Service Order Items)
- Fit-out installation with acoustic testing and finish quality standards
- MEP installation requiring COC and commissioning certification
- Retail fit-out with brand standard adherence and health department approvals
- IT/AV installation with network certification and integration testing
- Commissioning with performance testing and BMS integration verification

## Project-Specific Procurement Considerations

- **Office Buildings:** Tenant fit-out coordination, raised floor systems, acoustic ceiling grids, BMS integration, IT infrastructure
- **Retail Centers:** Brand-specific fit-out packages, refrigeration systems, signage programs, common area finishes, loading dock coordination
- **Hospitality:** FF&E global procurement, kitchen equipment packages, laundry equipment, spa equipment, brand-standard compliance
- **Mixed Commercial:** Diverse tenant requirements, phased delivery, common area vs tenant area procurement split
- **Refurbishments:** Phased occupancy, noise and dust management, existing MEP integration, heritage considerations
- **Green Building:** LEED/BREEAM/EDGE certification requirements, sustainable material sourcing, energy-efficient equipment

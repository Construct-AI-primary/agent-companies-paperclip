# Mining & Minerals Project Procurement Typology

## Sector Overview
Mining projects encompass open-pit, underground, processing plants, tailings storage facilities (TSF), heap leach, and mineral processing. Procurement involves heavy equipment, process equipment, and specialized services for mining operations.

## Common PO Types (Purchase Orders for Goods)

| PO Category | Typical Goods | Key Specifications | Lead Times |
|-------------|---------------|-------------------|------------|
| Mobile Equipment | Haul trucks, excavators, drill rigs, loaders | OEM specifications, Tier 4 emissions, mine-grade | 30-60 weeks |
| Stationary Equipment | Crushers, mills, screens, conveyors | FLSmidth, Metso, Weir OEM specs | 20-40 weeks |
| Process Equipment | Flotation cells, thickeners, filters | OEM specifications, metallurgical guarantees | 24-40 weeks |
| Structural Steel | Processing plant structures, conveyors | SABS/ASTM structural, galvanized/painted | 8-16 weeks |
| Conveyor Systems | Belting, idlers, pulleys, drives | SANS/ISO belt standards, fire-resistant belting | 12-24 weeks |
| Electrical | MV/LV switchgear, transformers, drives | IEC standards, hazardous area (dust) | 16-30 weeks |
| Instrumentation | Level, flow, density analyzers | ATEX for hazardous areas, SCADA integration | 12-20 weeks |

## Common SO Tracking Requirements

| Category | SO Focus Areas | Critical SO Verification Points |
|----------|---------------|--------------------------------|
| Mobile Equipment | Factory acceptance, delivery logistics | FAT, pre-delivery inspection, commissioning parts list |
| Process Equipment | Manufacturing milestones, testing | Performance test guarantees, material certificates |
| Conveyor Components | Belt supply, idler availability | Fire-resistant belting certification, belt joint specs |
| TSF Equipment | Geotextiles, pipes, valves | Geotextile specifications, pipe pressure ratings |

## Common WO Types (Work Orders for Services)

| WO Category | Typical Services | Compliance Requirements |
|-------------|-----------------|------------------------|
| Earthworks | Site preparation, bulk earthworks, TSF construction | Compaction testing, geotechnical approvals |
| Civil Construction | Foundations, structures, roads, drainage | Concrete specifications, structural inspections |
| Mining Services | Drilling, blasting, load & haul | Mine health & safety regulations, explosive certification |
| Plant Assembly | Steel erection, equipment alignment | Lifting plans, bolting torque records |
| Commissioning | Cold/hot commissioning, performance testing | Commissioning procedures, handover documentation |
| Camp and Logistics | Camp setup, transport, catering | Camp hygiene standards, transport permits |

## Key Goods vs Services Distinctions for Mining

### Goods (PO Items)
- Mobile equipment with OEM warranty and spare parts requirements
- Process equipment with metallurgical performance guarantees
- Conveyor belting with fire-resistant and anti-static certification
- Wear materials and consumables for ongoing operation

### Services (WO/Service Order Items)
- Earthworks with geotechnical and compaction requirements
- Mining contractor services with safety and production targets
- Commissioning with performance testing against guarantees
- Tailings dam construction with strict quality assurance

## Project-Specific Procurement Considerations
- **Open-Pit Mines:** Large earthmoving equipment, haul roads, blasting services
- **Underground Mines:** Ground support, ventilation, dewatering systems, shaft equipment
- **Processing Plants:** Crusher circuits, grinding mills, flotation circuits, thickeners
- **Tailings Storage:** Geotextiles, pipeline systems, dam construction, monitoring instrumentation
- **Remote Locations:** Camp infrastructure, fuel supply, helicopter transport, water treatment

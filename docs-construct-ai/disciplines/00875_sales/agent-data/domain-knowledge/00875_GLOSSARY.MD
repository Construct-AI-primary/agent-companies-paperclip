# Sales Glossary

## Key Terms

### business development
The process of identifying, nurturing, and acquiring new business opportunities to drive revenue growth.

### pipeline
The total value of all active sales opportunities being pursued.

### win rate
The percentage of opportunities pursued that result in a successful contract award.

### value proposition
The unique value and benefits that the organization offers to clients, expressed through differentiators and competitive advantages.

### proposal
A formal document submitted in response to an RFP, presenting the organization's solution, pricing, and qualifications.

### client relationship management
The practice of building and maintaining long-term relationships with clients to drive repeat business and referrals.

### sales cycle
The average time from initial qualification to contract award for an opportunity.

# Other Parties Glossary

## Key Terms

### stakeholder
Any individual, group, or organization that can affect or is affected by the project.

### regulatory liaison
The process of communicating and interacting with regulatory authorities to obtain permits, approvals, and ensure compliance.

### permit
An official authorization issued by a regulatory authority allowing specific activities or operations to proceed.

### third party
An external entity not directly part of the project organization but involved through agreements, services, or regulatory oversight.

### stakeholder engagement
The process of involving and communicating with stakeholders to understand their concerns and build support for the project.

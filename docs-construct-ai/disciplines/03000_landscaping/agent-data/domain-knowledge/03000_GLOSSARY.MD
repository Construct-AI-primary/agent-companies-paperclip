# Landscaping Glossary

## Key Terms

### soft landscaping
Plant-based landscape works including trees, shrubs, grasses, lawns, and ground covers.

### hard landscaping
Non-plant landscape elements including paving, walls, paths, steps, fences, and water features.

### irrigation
Artificial application of water to plants through a designed system of pipes, sprinklers, and emitters.

### topsoil
The upper layer of soil, rich in organic matter, used for planting and establishing vegetation.

### balled and burlapped (B&B)
Trees that have been dug from the ground with a ball of soil around their roots, wrapped in burlap for transport and planting.

### establishment period
The period after planting during which plants establish roots and require intensive care (watering, weeding, monitoring).

### hardscape drawing
Technical drawings showing the layout and construction details of paved areas, walls, and other non-vegetative landscape elements.

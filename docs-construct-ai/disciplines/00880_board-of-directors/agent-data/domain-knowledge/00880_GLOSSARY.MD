# Board of Directors Glossary

## Key Terms

### fiduciary duty
The legal obligation of directors to act in good faith, with due care, and in the best interests of the company and its shareholders.

### governance
The system of rules, practices, and processes by which an organization is directed and controlled.

### strategic oversight
The board's responsibility to guide and monitor the organization's long-term direction and performance.

### audit committee
A board committee responsible for overseeing financial reporting, internal controls, and external audit functions.

### duty of care
The obligation of directors to make informed decisions with the care that a prudent person would exercise in similar circumstances.

### duty of loyalty
The obligation of directors to act in the best interests of the company and avoid conflicts of interest.

### quorum
The minimum number of board members that must be present for meetings to be valid and decisions binding.

### proxy voting
The process by which a shareholder authorizes another person to vote their shares at a meeting.

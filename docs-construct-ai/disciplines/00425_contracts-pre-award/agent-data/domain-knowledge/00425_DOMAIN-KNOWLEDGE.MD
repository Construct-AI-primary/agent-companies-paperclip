# 00425 Contracts Pre-Award Domain Knowledge

## Part 1: Role on Large Projects
### 1.2 Contracts Pre-Award Team Structure and Roles

The Contracts Pre-Award team on large projects consists of multiple roles working collaboratively:

| Role | Level | Primary Focus | Reports To |
|------|-------|--------------|-----------|
| **Pre-Award Manager** | Senior | Tender management, procurement strategy | Contracts Director |
| **Bid Administrator** | Mid-level | Tender documentation, vendor communication | Pre-Award Manager |

**Note:** Director-level roles are handled separately under discipline 00883. This document focuses on the operational contracts pre-award team.


### 1.1 Executive Summary
The Contracts Pre-Award function manages the complete tendering lifecycle before contract execution. This includes tender creation, supplier invitation and management, secure submission handling, multi-criteria evaluation, award recommendation, and contract preparation.

### 1.2 Key Responsibilities

**Tender Creation & Management:**
- Develop tender specifications and documentation
- Define evaluation criteria and weighting
- Configure tender timelines and milestones
- Attach specifications and technical requirements

**Supplier Invitation & Management:**
- Identify and invite qualified suppliers
- Manage supplier communication and queries
- Track supplier response and engagement
- Pre-qualify suppliers against requirements

**Submission Handling:**
- Receive and log secure tender submissions
- Verify submission completeness
- Manage tender clarification process
- Maintain submission confidentiality

**Evaluation Framework:**
- Configure multi-criteria evaluation system
- Manage evaluation panel assignment
- Track evaluation progress and scoring
- Generate evaluation summaries

**Award Management:**
- Generate award recommendations
- Manage award approval workflow
- Handle award notifications and appeals
- Prepare contract documentation

## Part 2: Tender Lifecycle

### 2.1 Tender Stages

| Stage | Description | Key Activities |
|-------|-------------|----------------|
| Draft | Tender specification being prepared | Requirements gathering, evaluation criteria design |
| Published | Tender open for supplier response | Supplier notification, clarification management |
| Closed | Submission deadline passed | Submission verification, evaluation preparation |
| Evaluated | Evaluation complete | Scoring complete, recommendations generated |
| Awarded | Award decision made | Award notification, contract preparation |

### 2.2 Evaluation Criteria Types
- **Technical capability** — Relevant experience, methodology, resources
- **Commercial** — Pricing, payment terms, financial stability
- **Quality** — Quality management systems, track record
- **Schedule** — Delivery capability, programme compliance
- **Compliance** — Regulatory compliance, certifications

## Part 3: AI Agent Persona Definition

### 3.1 Core Identity
You are a Contracts Pre-Award professional managing tendering and procurement processes. You specialize in tender specification, supplier management, evaluation frameworks, and award processes.

### 3.2 Key Behaviors
**The agent should:**
- Ensure all tenders follow established procurement procedures
- Maintain confidentiality and fairness throughout the process
- Generate comprehensive documentation for audit compliance
- Track all communications and decisions for audit trail

**The agent should NOT:**
- Disclose confidential tender information between suppliers
- Modify evaluation scores without documented justification
- Recommend awards without complete evaluation

## Part 4: AI-Native Operations

### Part 4A: Text-Native vs Structured Data Classification

| Classification | Description | Examples | AI Pattern |
|---------------|-------------|----------|-----------|
| **Text-Native** | Tender specifications, evaluation reports, award recommendations, contract drafts, supplier communications | LLM-friendly: drafting, specification writing, report generation |
| **Structured Data** | Evaluation scores, supplier responses, tender timelines, pricing data, scoring matrices | Direct processing, analytics, scoring calculations |
| **Document-Native** | Technical drawings, specifications, submissions, compliance certificates | Document parsing, metadata extraction, structured data generation |
| **Human-Led Only** | Evaluation panel scoring, award decisions, supplier negotiations | Human execution, AI provides checklists, analysis, documentation |

### Part 4B: Automation Spectrum

| Automation Level | Definition | Examples | Human Role |
|-----------------|------------|---------|-----------|
| **Full Automation** | AI executes end-to-end with final human review | Tender template generation, submission completeness checks, evaluation framework drafting, award recommendation documentation, contract preparation from standard templates | Reviews and approves |
| **Augment AI + Human** | AI drafts, human validates and finalizes | Tender specification drafting, evaluation criteria weighting, supplier pre-qualification assessment, scoring analysis, contract non-standard terms | Co-creates, validates, decides |
| **Human-Led, AI-Informed** | AI alerts or recommends, human decides | Supplier selection for tender, evaluation panel scoring, award decision, appeal handling | Decides |
| **Human-Led Only** | AI has no role | Contract negotiations, supplier relationship management, final award sign-off | Executes and decides |

### Part 4C: Document Generation Pipeline

| Phase | Document Types | AI Trigger | Output Format |
|-------|---------------|------------|--------------|
| **Phase 1: Tender Preparation** | Tender specifications, evaluation criteria, supplier invitation templates, tender notices | Requirement received | PDF, structured forms, email |
| **Phase 2: Publication** | Tender notices, supplier communications, clarification responses, addenda | Tender published | PDF, email, web posting |
| **Phase 3: Evaluation** | Evaluation summaries, score analysis, compliance checks, recommendation reports | Submissions received | PDF, Excel, structured reports |
| **Phase 4: Award** | Award recommendations, contract drafts, notification letters, appeal responses | Evaluation complete | PDF, structured documents |

### Part 4D: AI-Native Capabilities Beyond Automation

| Capability Category | Pre-Award Examples |
|--------------------|---------------------------|
| **Predictive Intelligence** | Supplier response prediction, submission timeline forecasting, evaluation duration estimation |
| **Multi-Agent Orchestration** | Tender creation → Supplier invitation → Submission receipt → Evaluation → Award recommendation chain |
| **Natural Language Interface** | Tender specification drafting ("Draft a tender for structural steel supply"), evaluation criteria query, contract template generation |
| **Compliance Intelligence** | Procurement regulation compliance checking, audit trail generation, conflict of interest detection |

### Part 4E: AI Safety Boundaries

**Non-Delegable Human Decisions:**
1. Final tender publication approval
2. Supplier selection for tender invitation
3. Evaluation scoring by panel members
4. Award decision and recommendation approval
5. Contract terms negotiation
6. Appeal decisions
7. Conflict of interest determinations

**AI Must Always Disclose:**
1. When tender specifications may not meet procurement regulations
2. When evaluation criteria weights require panel approval
3. When supplier responses are incomplete or non-compliant
4. When conflicts of interest may exist
5. What procurement regulations may apply to the tender
6. What information must remain confidential

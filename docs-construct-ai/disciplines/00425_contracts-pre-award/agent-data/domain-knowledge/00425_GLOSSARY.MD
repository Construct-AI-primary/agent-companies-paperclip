# 00425 Contracts Pre-Award Glossary

## Key Terms

**Tender** — A formal invitation to suppliers to submit proposals for goods, services, or works under specified terms and conditions.

**Tender Specification** — The detailed document describing requirements, scope, technical specifications, and evaluation criteria for a tender.

**Evaluation Criteria** — Defined standards against which tender submissions are assessed and scored (technical, commercial, quality, schedule, compliance).

**Award Recommendation** — The formal recommendation from the evaluation panel to the decision-making authority identifying the preferred tenderer.

**Tender Security / Bid Bond** — Financial guarantee submitted by tenderer to ensure commitment to the tender process.

**Clarification** — Formal questions and responses between the tenderer and procuring entity during the tender period.

**Addendum** — A modification or clarification issued during the tender period that forms part of the tender documentation.

**Submission Compliance** — The degree to which a tender submission meets all mandatory requirements of the tender specification.

**Conflict of Interest** — A situation where a person or entity involved in the tender process has competing interests that could compromise fairness.

**Standstill Period** — A mandatory waiting period between award notification and contract signature to allow for appeals.

## Acronyms

| Acronym | Full Form | Context |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| RFP | Request for Proposal | Procurement method where supplier proposes methodology and price |
| RFQ | Request for Quotation | Procurement method for standard goods or services |
| ITT | Invitation to Tender | Formal invitation for tenders |
| EOI | Expression of Interest | Pre-qualification stage for tenderers |
| BOQ | Bill of Quantities | Detailed schedule of quantities for pricing |
| LOA | Letter of Award | Formal notification of tender award |
| LOI | Letter of Intent | Pre-contract indication of intention to award |
| NDA | Non-Disclosure Agreement | Confidentiality agreement for tender information |
| SLA | Service Level Agreement | Service requirements and performance standards |
| QMS | Quality Management System | Supplier's quality management framework |

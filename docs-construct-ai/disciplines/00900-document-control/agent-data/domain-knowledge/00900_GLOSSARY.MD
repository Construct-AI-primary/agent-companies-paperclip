# Document Control Glossary

## Key Terms

### EDMS (Electronic Document Management System)
A software system used to store, manage, and track electronic documents and scanned images of paper documents.

### transmittal
A formal document used to transmit documents or deliverables from one party to another, with record of sending and receipt.

### revision
A subsequent version of a document issued to incorporate changes or corrections from a previous version.

### submittal
A document, sample, or item submitted by a contractor for review and approval by the project team prior to use or installation.

### RFI (Request for Information)
A formal query raised during construction to seek clarification or decisions on technical or design matters.

### document register
A comprehensive list of all project documents with their status, revision, and distribution information.

### records management
The systematic control of records throughout their lifecycle, including creation, maintenance, use, and disposition.

### as-built drawing
A drawing updated to reflect the actual constructed configuration of a project, incorporating all changes made during construction.

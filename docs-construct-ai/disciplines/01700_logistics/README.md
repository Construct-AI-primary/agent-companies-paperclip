# 01700 Logistics Discipline

## Overview

The Logistics discipline encompasses the comprehensive management of supply chain operations, transportation coordination, inventory optimization, and warehouse management systems. This discipline ensures efficient movement of goods, materials, and resources throughout the organization.

## Core Components

### Supply Chain Management
- End-to-end supply chain visibility and optimization
- Multi-modal transportation coordination
- Supplier performance monitoring and analytics

### Inventory Management
- Stock level optimization and replenishment
- Demand forecasting and capacity planning
- Warehouse operations and tracking

### Transportation Management
- Fleet coordination and route optimization
- Customs and compliance management
- Real-time tracking and monitoring

## Key Processes

### Contractor Vetting
- Supplier qualification and evaluation
- Performance monitoring and scoring
- Risk assessment and compliance checking

### Workflow Configuration
- Import/export process management
- Customs clearance procedures
- Documentation and compliance workflows

### Data Capture and Analysis
- Procurement data integration
- Workflow optimization reporting
- Performance analytics and insights

## Integration Points

### Related Disciplines
- **01900 Procurement**: Supplier management and order fulfillment
- **00435 Contracts**: Service level agreements and performance monitoring
- **01200 Finance**: Cost optimization and budget planning

### Technical Integration
- ERP system connectivity
- Transportation provider APIs
- Warehouse management systems
- Real-time tracking platforms

# Public Relations Glossary

## Key Terms

### media relations
The practice of building and maintaining relationships with journalists and media outlets to secure positive coverage and manage the flow of information to the public.

### stakeholder engagement
The process of involving individuals, groups, or organizations who may affect or be affected by the project in communications and decision-making activities.

### community engagement
The process of working collaboratively with community groups and residents to address issues that affect their wellbeing and build relationships based on trust and mutual respect.

### crisis communications
The strategic management of communications during a crisis to protect the organization's reputation and ensure accurate information reaches stakeholders quickly.

### press release
An official statement issued to the news media to announce newsworthy information about the project.

### holding statement
A preliminary statement issued during an emerging situation before all facts are known, acknowledging the situation and committing to provide updates.

### key messages
Core talking points that convey the organization's position, priorities, and responses to important issues.

### media monitoring
The systematic tracking and analysis of media coverage to identify trends, issues, and opportunities relevant to the project.

# Development Glossary

## Key Terms

### project origination
The process of identifying, assessing, and initiating new project opportunities.

### feasibility study
A comprehensive assessment of a project's technical, commercial, environmental, and financial viability.

### financial close
The point at which all project agreements are executed and financing is secured, allowing construction to begin.

### offtake agreement
A long-term agreement to purchase a project's output, providing revenue certainty.

### ESIA (Environmental and Social Impact Assessment)
A comprehensive assessment of environmental and social impacts of a proposed project.

### Final Investment Decision (FID)
The formal decision by project sponsors to commit capital and proceed with construction.

### stakeholder engagement
The process of consulting and building relationships with parties affected by or interested in the project.

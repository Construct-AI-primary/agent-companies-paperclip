# Governance Glossary

## Key Terms

### corporate governance
The system of rules, practices, and processes by which an organization is directed and controlled, ensuring accountability, transparency, and fairness.

### delegated authority
The formal assignment of decision-making power to individuals or committees within defined limits, as documented in a delegation matrix.

### board pack
A comprehensive set of documents and reports prepared for a board of directors meeting, including financial reports, risk updates, and project status.

### policy register
A central record of all organizational policies, including ownership, approval dates, review dates, and status.

### quorum
The minimum number of members required to be present at a meeting for decisions to be valid.

### steering committee
A senior-level committee responsible for strategic oversight and decision-making on a project or programme.

### governance framework
The structured system of governance principles, policies, procedures, and structures that guide organizational decision-making and oversight.

### compliance
Adherence to applicable laws, regulations, policies, and standards governing the organization's activities.

# Inspection Glossary

## Key Terms

### hold point
A stage in work beyond which the activity cannot proceed until inspection and acceptance is granted by the designated authority.

### witness point
A stage where the inspecting party is notified to attend. Work may proceed if the inspector does not attend.

### Inspection and Test Plan (ITP)
A document defining inspection and test activities, acceptance criteria, and responsibilities for a specific work process.

### material test report (MTR)
A document certifying the chemical and mechanical properties of a material, typically matched to delivered material via heat or batch numbers.

### positive material identification (PMI)
Verification that an alloy material contains the specified composition of elements, typically using portable XRF or OES instruments.

### dimensional control
Verification that installed components meet specified positions, levels, alignments, and tolerances.

### non-destructive testing (NDT)
Examination methods used to detect flaws in materials and welds without damaging the component.

### dry film thickness (DFT)
The measured thickness of a cured coating or paint layer, verified to meet specification requirements.

### calibration
Verification and adjustment of measurement equipment against a known standard to ensure accuracy.

# Contracts Post-Award Domain Knowledge

## Part 1: Role on Large Projects

### 1.1 Executive Summary
The Contracts Post-Award team provides operational support for project delivery, managing contract administration, compliance tracking, and coordination with main contractors.

### 1.2 Contracts Post-Award Team Structure and Roles

| Role | Level | Primary Focus | Reports To |
|------|-------|--------------|-----------|
| **Team Lead** | Senior | Team oversight, stakeholder coordination | 00883 |
| **Coordinator** | Mid-level | Task coordination, record keeping | Team Lead |
| **Administrator** | Entry-level | Document control, data entry | Coordinator |

**Note:** Director-level roles handled separately.

## Part 2: Tasks and Responsibilities
- Contract administration and compliance monitoring
- Document control and records management
- Coordination with project stakeholders
- Reporting and data management

# Security Glossary

## Key Terms

### access control
Procedures and systems that regulate who or what can enter or use a protected area, system, or resource.

### closed-circuit television (CCTV)
A video surveillance system where cameras are connected to a closed network for monitoring and recording, used for security purposes.

### threat assessment
The systematic evaluation of potential threats to personnel, assets, or operations, considering likelihood and potential impact.

### layered security
A security approach using multiple protective barriers at different levels so that if one fails, others remain in place.

### intrusion detection
Systems and sensors that detect unauthorized physical or digital entry into a protected area.

### security incident
Any event that compromises or threatens the security of personnel, assets, information, or the site, including theft, unauthorized access, vandalism, and threats.

### visitor management
The process of registering, authorizing, tracking, and escorting non-personnel visitors on site.

### perimeter security
Physical and technological measures protecting the boundary of a site from unauthorized access.

### gate pass
A document authorizing the removal of materials or equipment from site, verified by security at exit points.

### CPTED (Crime Prevention Through Environmental Design)
A multi-disciplinary approach to deterring criminal behavior through environmental design, including natural surveillance, territorial reinforcement, and access management.

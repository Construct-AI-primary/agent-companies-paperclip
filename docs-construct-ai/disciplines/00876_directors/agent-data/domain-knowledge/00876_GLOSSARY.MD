# Directors Glossary

## Key Terms

### director
A member of the board of directors or an executive director with responsibility for strategic leadership and organizational oversight.

### board of directors
The governing body elected by shareholders to oversee the organization's activities and protect shareholder interests.

### strategic planning
The process of defining organizational direction, setting goals, and allocating resources to achieve long-term objectives.

### corporate governance
The system of rules, practices, and processes by which an organization is directed and controlled.

### fiduciary duty
The legal obligation of directors and officers to act in the best interests of the organization and its shareholders.

### executive oversight
The responsibility of directors to monitor and guide the organization's operations and performance.

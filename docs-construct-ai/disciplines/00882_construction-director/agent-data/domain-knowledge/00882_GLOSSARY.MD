# Construction Director Glossary

## Key Terms

### construction strategy
The overall approach to executing and delivering construction work, including methodology, sequencing, and resource planning.

### programme delivery
The management and oversight of a construction programme to achieve its objectives safely, on time, and within budget.

### escalation framework
The structured process for raising issues through management levels for resolution.

### construction execution plan
A comprehensive document defining how construction will be planned, executed, monitored, and controlled.

### recovery schedule
A revised construction schedule developed to recover lost time and return to the baseline programme.

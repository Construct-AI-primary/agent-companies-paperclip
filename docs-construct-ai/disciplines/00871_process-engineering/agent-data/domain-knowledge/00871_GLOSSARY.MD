# Process Engineering Glossary

## Key Terms

### process flow diagram (PFD)
A schematic diagram showing the major process equipment, flows, and operating conditions in a chemical or industrial process.

### piping and instrumentation diagram (P&ID)
A detailed diagram showing all piping, instrumentation, control systems, and equipment for a process system.

### mass balance
An accounting of all mass inputs, outputs, and accumulation in a process system.

### energy balance
An accounting of all energy inputs, outputs, and losses in a process system.

### HAZOP (Hazard and Operability Study)
A systematic method for identifying process hazards and operability problems using guide words and process parameters.

### debottlenecking
The process of identifying and removing constraints that limit process capacity or throughput.

### NPSH (Net Positive Suction Head)
The absolute pressure at a pump suction minus the liquid vapour pressure. Critical for preventing cavitation.

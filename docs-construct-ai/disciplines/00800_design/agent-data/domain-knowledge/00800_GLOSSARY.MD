# Design Management Glossary

## Key Terms

### design execution plan (DEP)
A document describing how design will be managed, organized, and delivered on a project, including roles, schedule, standards, deliverables, and quality control processes.

### interdisciplinary review (IDR)
A coordinated review of a design deliverable involving all engineering disciplines to ensure interfaces are properly addressed and no conflicts exist.

### clash detection
The process of identifying spatial conflicts between different discipline models in a BIM environment.

### FEED (Front-End Engineering Design)
The design phase following conceptual design, in which sufficient engineering detail is developed to define the project scope, layout, equipment, and a firm cost estimate.

### BIM (Building Information Modeling)
A process involving the generation and management of digital representations of physical and functional characteristics of a project.

### as-built
Drawings updated to reflect the actual constructed configuration, incorporating all changes made during construction.

### shop drawing
Detailed drawings prepared by a contractor or fabricator to show how they intend to construct elements, submitted for design team review.

### RFI (Request for Information)
A formal query raised during construction to seek design clarification or decisions from the engineering team.

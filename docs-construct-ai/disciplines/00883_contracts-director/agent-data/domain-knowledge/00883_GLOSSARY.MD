# Contracts Director Glossary

## Key Terms

### contract strategy
The overall approach to structuring, procuring, and administering contracts on a project to optimize commercial outcomes and risk allocation.

### commercial oversight
The executive-level monitoring and management of contractual and commercial performance across a project or portfolio.

### dispute resolution
The process of resolving contractual disputes through negotiation, mediation, adjudication, or arbitration.

### risk allocation
The distribution of contractual risks between contracting parties based on which party is best positioned to manage each risk.

### contract variation
A change to the scope, price, or terms of a contract, typically requiring formal agreement between parties.

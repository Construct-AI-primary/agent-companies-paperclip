# Local Content Glossary

## Key Terms

### local content
The extent to which a project utilizes local/national goods, services, labor, and enterprise development to maximize economic and social benefits to the host country or community.

### local procurement
The procurement of goods and services from local or national suppliers, measured as a percentage of total procurement expenditure.

### local employment
The employment of local or national citizens in project roles, tracked against specified local employment targets.

### skills transfer
The process of transferring knowledge and skills from expatriate or experienced personnel to local/national employees.

### enterprise development
Programmes and initiatives designed to develop the capacity and capability of local businesses to participate in the project supply chain.

### expatriate
A foreign national employed on a project, typically used to compare against local/national workforce ratios.

### local supplier
A business registered and operating within the host country or local community that supplies goods or services to the project.

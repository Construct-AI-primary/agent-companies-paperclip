# MINROAD-005: Haul Road Design

## Issue Summary

Develop comprehensive haul road design for mining operations. This phase includes heavy vehicle design standards, gradient optimization, surface design, and maintenance requirements.

## Objectives

- Design haul roads for heavy mining vehicles
- Optimize gradients and alignment
- Specify surface requirements
- Develop maintenance procedures

## Scope of Work

### 1. Traffic Analysis
- Vehicle type and loading
- Traffic volume projections
- Load spectrum analysis
- Design life determination

### 2. Geometric Design
- Design speed and width
- Gradient optimization
- Curve design and superelevation
- Intersection design

### 3. Surface Design
- Subgrade preparation
- Pavement structure
- Wearing course materials
- Compaction requirements

### 4. Drainage and Safety
- Crown and crossfall
- Side drains and culverts
- Safety barriers
- Lighting requirements

## Deliverables

1. **Haul Road Design Report**
2. **Alignment Plans**
3. **Surface Specifications**
4. **Drainage Design**
5. **Maintenance Plan**

# MINROAD-001: Site Assessment & Feasibility

## Issue Summary

Conduct comprehensive site assessment and feasibility analysis for mining support civil works and road/highway construction projects. This critical first phase evaluates site conditions, technical feasibility, environmental considerations, and provides the foundation for all subsequent design and construction activities.

## Objectives

- Evaluate site conditions for mining/road civil construction
- Assess physical, environmental, and regulatory site constraints
- Conduct geotechnical investigation and foundation assessments
- Develop preliminary cost estimates and feasibility recommendations
- Prepare site layout optimization concepts

## Scope of Work

### 1. Topographic and Site Survey

#### Survey Activities
- **Control Survey**: Establish survey control network
- **Topographic Mapping**: Detailed topographic survey of site area
- **Boundary Survey**: Identify property boundaries and easements
- **Utility Survey**: Locate existing utilities and infrastructure

#### Data Collection
- Elevation data with 0.1m accuracy
- Natural and cultural features mapping
- Drainage patterns and water bodies
- Existing access routes and structures

### 2. Geotechnical Investigation

#### Soil Analysis Program
- **Test Pits**: Excavation and logging of test pits
- **Borings**: SPT borings at design-critical locations
- **Laboratory Testing**: Soil classification, strength, and compaction tests
- **Groundwater Assessment**: Water table monitoring

#### Foundation Assessment
- Allowable bearing capacity determination
- Settlement analysis for structures
- Slope stability considerations
- Seismic site classification

### 3. Environmental Scoping

#### Environmental Constraints
- Wetland and waterway identification
- Protected species habitat assessment
- Archaeological and cultural resource surveys
- Community impact considerations

#### permitting Requirements
- Environmental impact assessment scoping
- Permit timeline development
- Stakeholder engagement planning
- Mitigation requirement identification

### 4. Mining-Specific Considerations

#### Mining Concession Requirements
- Client-specific standards compliance
- GAC/EGA-style safety requirements
- Environmental control plan alignment
- Production chain integration

#### Haul Road Assessment
- Heavy vehicle traffic projections
- Gradient and alignment feasibility
- Ground conditions for heavy loads
- Drainage requirements

### 5. Feasibility Analysis

#### Technical Feasibility
- Alignment options development
- Earthworks balance optimization
- Structure type evaluation
- Material source identification

#### Economic Feasibility
- Preliminary cost estimates (±20%)
- Construction schedule development
- Risk assessment and mitigation
- Go/no-go recommendation

## Deliverables

### Primary Deliverables
1. **Site Assessment Report**
   - Executive summary with key findings
   - Topographic survey results
   - Site constraint analysis

2. **Geotechnical Report**
   - Soil investigation results
   - Foundation recommendations
   - Construction considerations

3. **Environmental Scoping Report**
   - Preliminary impact assessment
   - Permitting requirements
   - Mitigation recommendations

4. **Feasibility Study**
   - Technical feasibility assessment
   - Preliminary cost estimates
   - Risk register and mitigation

## Technical Requirements

### Survey Standards
- Survey accuracy: 0.1m for elevations
- GIS coordinate system: UTM/local projection
- Data format: CAD/GIS compatible

### Geotechnical Standards
- ASTM standards for soil testing
- AASHTO guidelines for pavement investigation
- Client-specific requirements

### Analysis Tools
- CAD software for plan preparation
- GIS for site analysis
- Earthworks software for cut-fill optimization
- Financial modeling for cost estimates

## Success Criteria

- Comprehensive site coverage
- Accurate geotechnical data collection
- Clear feasibility recommendation
- Regulatory pathway identified
- Realistic cost and schedule estimates

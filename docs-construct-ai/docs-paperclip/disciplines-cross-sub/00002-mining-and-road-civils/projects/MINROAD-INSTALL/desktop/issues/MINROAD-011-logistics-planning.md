# MINROAD-011: Logistics & Fleet Management

## Issue Summary

Implement logistics and fleet management for mining and road civil projects. This phase includes fleet scheduling, fuel management, equipment tracking, and maintenance planning.

## Objectives

- Optimize fleet utilization
- Manage fuel consumption
- Track equipment performance
- Schedule maintenance activities

## Scope of Work

### 1. Fleet Management
- Fleet composition optimization
- Dispatch scheduling
- Utilization tracking
- Productivity analysis

### 2. Fuel Management
- Fuel consumption tracking
- Ordering and delivery
- Inventory management
- Cost reporting

### 3. Equipment Tracking
- GPS tracking systems
- Hour meter monitoring
- Performance reporting
- Idle time analysis

## Deliverables

1. **Fleet Schedule**
2. **Fuel Management System**
3. **Equipment Tracking Reports**
4. **Maintenance Schedule**

# MINROAD-003: Road Design & Alignment

## Issue Summary

Develop comprehensive road design and alignment for mining and road civil projects. This phase includes horizontal and vertical alignment, pavement structure design, drainage systems, and detailed cross-sections.

## Objectives

- Develop optimal horizontal and vertical alignment
- Design pavement structure and materials
- Specify drainage requirements
- Prepare construction details and quantities

## Scope of Work

### 1. Horizontal Alignment Design
- Route selection and optimization
- Design speed and geometric standards
- Superelevation design
- Intersection and access design

### 2. Vertical Alignment Design
- Profile design and grade optimization
- Vertical curve design (crest/sag)
- Grade transition design
- Drainage considerations

### 3. Pavement Design
- Traffic loading analysis
- Subgrade evaluation
- Pavement structure design
- Material specifications

### 4. Drainage Design
- Surface drainage layout
- Culvert and bridge sizing
- Side drains and channels
- Erosion protection

## Deliverables

1. **Alignment Design Package**
2. **Pavement Design Report**
3. **Drainage Design Plans**
4. **Construction Details**
5. **Quantity Takeoff**

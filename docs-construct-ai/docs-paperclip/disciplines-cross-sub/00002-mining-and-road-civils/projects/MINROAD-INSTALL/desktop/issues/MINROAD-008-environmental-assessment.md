# MINROAD-008: Environmental Impact & Permitting

## Issue Summary

Develop comprehensive environmental impact assessment and permitting for mining and road civil projects. This phase includes EIA documentation, permit applications, and environmental management plans.

## Objectives

- Assess environmental impacts
- Develop mitigation measures
- Obtain required permits
- Establish monitoring programs

## Scope of Work

### 1. Environmental Impact Assessment
- Air quality impacts
- Water resource impacts
- Soil and vegetation impacts
- Social and community impacts

### 2. Permitting Requirements
- Environmental permits
- Water permits
- Waste permits
- Construction permits

### 3. Environmental Management
- Mitigation plans
- Monitoring programs
- Rehabilitation plans
- Closure plans

## Deliverables

1. **Environmental Impact Assessment**
2. **Permit Applications**
3. **Environmental Management Plan**
4. **Monitoring Program**

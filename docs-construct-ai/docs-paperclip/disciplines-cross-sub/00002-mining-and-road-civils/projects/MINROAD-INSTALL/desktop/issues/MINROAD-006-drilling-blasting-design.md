# MINROAD-006: Drilling & Blasting Design

## Issue Summary

Develop comprehensive drilling and blasting design for mining and road civil projects. This phase includes blast pattern design, vibration monitoring, safety protocols, and fragmentation optimization.

## Objectives

- Design blast patterns for optimal fragmentation
- Minimize vibration and flyrock effects
- Ensure safety compliance
- Optimize blasting costs

## Scope of Work

### 1. Blast Design Parameters
- Drill pattern optimization
- Charge weight calculations
- Delay timing design
- Initiation sequence

### 2. Vibration Analysis
- Ground vibration prediction
- Air blast assessment
- Structural response analysis
- Monitoring requirements

### 3. Safety Protocols
- Blast exclusion zones
- Warning systems
- Post-blast inspection
- Emergency procedures

## Deliverables

1. **Blast Design Report**
2. **Vibration Predictions**
3. **Safety Plan**
4. **Fragmentation Assessment**

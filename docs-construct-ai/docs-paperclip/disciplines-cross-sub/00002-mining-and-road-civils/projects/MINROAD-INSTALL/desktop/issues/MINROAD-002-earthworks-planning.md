# MINROAD-002: Earthworks Planning & Optimization

## Issue Summary

Develop comprehensive earthworks execution plan for mining and road civil projects. This phase includes cut-fill optimization, equipment selection, production rate estimation, and ground improvement specifications to ensure efficient and cost-effective earthwork operations.

## Objectives

- Optimize cut-fill balance and material utilization
- Select appropriate equipment for earthwork operations
- Estimate production rates and construction schedules
- Specify ground improvement requirements
- Develop earthworks execution methodology

## Scope of Work

### 1. Cut-Fill Analysis and Balance

#### Mass Haul Calculations
- **Volume Calculations**: Calculate cut and fill volumes
- **Borrow Pit Design**: Identify and design borrow areas
- **Waste Areas**: Design spoil disposal locations
- **Balance Optimization**: Minimize haul distances

#### Material Properties
- **Shrinkage/Swell Factors**: Account for material changes
- **Compaction Requirements**: Determine target densities
- **Moisture Content**: Optimize for compaction
- **Material Classification**: Classify excavated materials

### 2. Equipment Selection and Planning

#### Heavy Equipment Fleet
- **Excavators**: Type and size selection
- **Haul Trucks**: Capacity and fleet size
- **Dozers**: Push/pull capacity
- **Graders**: Finishing and slope work

#### Production Rates
- **Hourly Production**: Estimate cubic meters/hour
- **Fleet Productivity**: Calculate combined output
- **Cycle Times**: Analyze loading, hauling, dumping
- **Weather Factors**: Adjust for conditions

### 3. Ground Improvement Requirements

#### Soil Stabilization
- **Mechanical Stabilization**: Compaction requirements
- **Chemical Stabilization**: Lime/cement treatment
- **Geosynthetics**: Reinforcement specifications
- **Drainage Improvements**: Preloading, wick drains

#### Slope Stability Measures
- **Cut Slope Design**: Angle and benching
- **Fill Slope Design**: Steepness and buttressing
- **Retention Systems**: Retaining structures
- **Erosion Protection**: Slope armoring

### 4. Mining-Specific Earthworks

#### Overburden Removal
- **Stripping Operations**: Progressive stripping plan
- **Stockpile Management**: Location and stacking
- **Haul Road Design**: Access road grades
- **Pit Bench Design**: Excavation sequencing

#### Haul Road Construction
- **Subgrade Preparation**: Compaction requirements
- **Wearing Course**: Material specifications
- **Drainage**: Crown and side drains
- **Maintenance Plan**: Repair procedures

## Deliverables

### Primary Deliverables
1. **Earthworks Execution Plan**
   - Cut-fill balance calculations
   - Equipment schedule
   - Production rate estimates
   - Construction sequence

2. **Ground Improvement Specifications**
   - Stabilization methods
   - Material requirements
   - Quality control criteria
   - Construction procedures

3. **Equipment Plan**
   - Fleet composition
   - Operating hours
   - Fuel requirements
   - Maintenance schedule

## Technical Requirements

### Calculation Standards
- AASHTO earthwork calculations
- Industry-standard production rates
- Fleet efficiency factors
- Weather adjustment factors

### Documentation
- Mass diagram preparation
- Haul diagram development
- Equipment utilization reports
- Cost breakdown by activity

## Success Criteria

- Optimized cut-fill balance achieved
- Equipment selection validated
- Realistic production rates established
- Ground improvement requirements defined
- Construction schedule feasible

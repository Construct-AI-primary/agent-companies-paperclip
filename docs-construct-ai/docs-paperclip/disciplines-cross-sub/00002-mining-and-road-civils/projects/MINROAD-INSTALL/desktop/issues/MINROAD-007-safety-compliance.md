# MINROAD-007: Safety & Compliance Requirements

## Issue Summary

Develop comprehensive safety and compliance requirements for mining and road civil projects. This phase includes hazard identification, risk assessment, safety planning, and HSE compliance documentation.

## Objectives

- Identify all project hazards
- Assess and mitigate risks
- Develop safety plans
- Ensure regulatory compliance

## Scope of Work

### 1. Hazard Identification
- Site hazard assessment
- Equipment hazards
- Material hazards
- Environmental hazards

### 2. Risk Assessment
- Probability and impact analysis
- Risk matrix development
- Mitigation strategies
- Residual risk evaluation

### 3. Safety Planning
- Safety management plan
- PPE requirements
- Training requirements
- Emergency response procedures

## Deliverables

1. **Safety Management Plan**
2. **Risk Assessment Register**
3. **Emergency Response Plan**
4. **HSE Compliance Documentation**

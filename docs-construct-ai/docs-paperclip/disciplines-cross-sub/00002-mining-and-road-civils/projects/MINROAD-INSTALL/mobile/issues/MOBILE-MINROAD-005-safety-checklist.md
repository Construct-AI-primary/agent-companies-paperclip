# MOBILE-MINROAD-005: Safety Checklist

## Issue Summary

Develop mobile safety checklist application for mining and road civil projects. This app enables field safety inspections, PPE compliance verification, and hazard reporting.

## Objectives

- Conduct field safety inspections
- Verify PPE compliance
- Report hazards
- Track safety incidents

## Scope of Work

### 1. Safety Inspections
- Pre-work checklists
- Equipment safety checks
- Site hazard assessments
- Compliance verification

### 2. PPE Compliance
- PPE requirements by task
- Compliance tracking
- Training verification
- Equipment inspection

### 3. Hazard Reporting
- Hazard identification
- Photo documentation
- Risk classification
- Mitigation tracking

## Deliverables

1. **Mobile Safety App**
2. **Inspection Checklists**
3. **PPE Compliance Module**
4. **Hazard Reporting System**

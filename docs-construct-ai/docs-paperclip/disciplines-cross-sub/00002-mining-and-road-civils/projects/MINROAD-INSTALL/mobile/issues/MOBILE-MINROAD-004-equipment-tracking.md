# MOBILE-MINROAD-004: Equipment Tracking

## Issue Summary

Develop mobile equipment tracking application for mining and road civil projects. This app enables fleet monitoring, fuel tracking, and utilization reporting.

## Objectives

- Track equipment location
- Monitor fuel consumption
- Report equipment utilization
- Analyze productivity

## Scope of Work

### 1. Fleet Monitoring
- GPS tracking
- Real-time location
- Route history
- Geofencing

### 2. Fuel Management
- Fuel consumption tracking
- Refueling records
- Fuel efficiency analysis
- Inventory management

### 3. Utilization Reporting
- Operating hours
- Idle time tracking
- Productivity metrics
- Performance dashboards

## Deliverables

1. **Mobile Tracking App**
2. **Fleet Monitoring Module**
3. **Fuel Management Module**
4. **Reporting Dashboard**

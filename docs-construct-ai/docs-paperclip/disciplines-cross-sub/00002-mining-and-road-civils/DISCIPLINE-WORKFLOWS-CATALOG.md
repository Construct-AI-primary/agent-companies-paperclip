# Mining and Road Civils Discipline Workflows Master Catalog

## Overview

This document catalogs all mining and road civil construction workflows derived from multiple engineering disciplines. Mining and road civil projects require comprehensive coordination across civil engineering, geotechnical, structural, safety, environmental, QA/QC, logistics, and security disciplines to ensure safe, compliant, and efficient infrastructure development.

## Mining and Road Civils Workflow Integration Matrix

### MINROAD-001: Site Assessment & Feasibility (Civil + Geotechnical + Environmental)

**Primary Disciplines**: Civil (00850), Geotechnical (00855), Environmental (01000)

**Integrated Workflows**:
- **CIVIL-001**: Stormwater Management Design → Mining/road site drainage analysis
- **GEOTECH-001**: Foundation Design → Ground condition assessment for foundations
- **ENV-001**: Environmental Impact Assessment → Mining/road environmental scoping

**Key Deliverables**:
- Site assessment report
- Geotechnical investigation summary
- Environmental impact screening
- Preliminary cost estimates
- Feasibility recommendations

### MINROAD-002: Earthworks Planning & Optimization (Civil + Geotechnical)

**Primary Disciplines**: Civil (00850), Geotechnical (00855)

**Integrated Workflows**:
- **CIVIL-003**: Earthworks Specification → Cut-fill optimization and balance
- **GEOTECH-004**: Earthworks & Ground Improvement → Ground improvement for earthworks
- **GEOTECH-005**: Geotechnical Construction Support → Earthworks monitoring

**Key Deliverables**:
- Earthworks execution plan
- Cut-fill balance calculations
- Ground improvement specifications
- Equipment requirements
- Production rate estimates

### MINROAD-003: Road Design & Alignment (Civil + Structural)

**Primary Disciplines**: Civil (00850), Structural (00872)

**Integrated Workflows**:
- **CIVIL-004**: Roadway Design → Highway and urban road alignment
- **CIVIL-005**: Pavement Design → Pavement structure and materials
- **STRUCT-001**: Bridge Design → Minor structures and culverts

**Key Deliverables**:
- Horizontal and vertical alignment
- Pavement design calculations
- Drainage design
- Cross-section details
- Quantities and cost estimates

### MINROAD-004: Bridge & Structure Design (Structural + Civil + Geotechnical)

**Primary Disciplines**: Structural (00872), Civil (00850), Geotechnical (00855)

**Integrated Workflows**:
- **STRUCT-001**: Bridge Design → Major bridge structures
- **STRUCT-002**: Foundation Design → Bridge foundations
- **CIVIL-006**: Civil Works Construction Support → Structure construction

**Key Deliverables**:
- Structural calculations
- Foundation design
- Reinforcement details
- Construction sequences
- Load ratings

### MINROAD-005: Haul Road Design (Civil + Geotechnical + Logistics)

**Primary Disciplines**: Civil (00850), Geotechnical (00855), Logistics (01700)

**Integrated Workflows**:
- **CIVIL-004**: Roadway Design → Heavy vehicle haul roads
- **GEOTECH-003**: Slope Stability Analysis → Haul road embankments
- **LOG-001**: Fleet Management → Heavy equipment routing

**Key Deliverables**:
- Haul road alignment
- Gradient and width specifications
- Surface design for heavy loads
- Drainage and erosion control
- Maintenance requirements

### MINROAD-006: Drilling & Blasting Design (Civil + Geotechnical + Safety)

**Key Deliverables**:

## Cross-Discipline Dependencies

### Primary Integration Points
1. **Civil Engineering (00850)** coordinates all mining/road workflows
2. **Quality Control (02250)** interfaces with all construction activities
3. **Safety (02400)** integrates with all field operations
4. **Logistics (01700)** coordinates with earthworks and construction
5. **Environmental (01000)** interfaces with civil and construction activities

### Workflow Integration Points
Each mining/road workflow documents:
- **Upstream inputs** from other disciplines
- **Downstream outputs** to dependent workflows
- **Cross-discipline interfaces** and coordination requirements
- **Key handoff points** between disciplines

# Mining and Road Civils Knowledge Base

## Overview

This document compiles the core knowledge base for mining and road civil construction projects, derived from industry best practices and GPC-style integrated service provider operations.

## Core Activity Domains

### Mining Support Civil Works

#### Land Preparation
- Vegetation clearing and grubbing
- Topsoil removal and stockpiling
- Overburden stripping and removal
- Site grading and levelling

#### Drilling & Blasting
- Drill pattern design and optimization
- Explosive loading and initiation
- Blast vibration monitoring
- Fragmentation control and assessment

#### Haulage & Crushing
- Load and haul operations
- Primary and secondary crushing
- Stockpile management
- Railhead/port logistics

#### Mine-Site Infrastructure
- Internal haul road construction
- Stockpile pad preparation
- Drainage channel installation
- Access route development

### Road & Highway Construction

#### Highway Projects
- Major highway construction
- Regional road development
- Urban road asphalting
- Cross-border corridor projects

#### Road-Corridor Works
- Earthworks and embankments
- Culvert and drainage installation
- Sub-base and base-course placement
- Asphalt pavement layers

#### Bridge & Structure Works
- Bridge pier construction
- Abutment and wingwall construction
- Deck slab casting
- Retaining wall systems

## Activity Overlap Matrix

| Activity | Mining-Civil | Road-Civil |
|----------|-------------|------------|
| Earthworks | Overburden removal, pit-bench-cutting | Cut-fill, embankments, subgrade preparation |
| Road Creation | Internal haul-roads, access tracks | Public highways, urban roads, bridges |
| Drainage | Run-off channels, mine-site ditches | Road-side drains, culverts, drainage networks |
| Logistics | High-tonnage haul to railhead, fuel depots | Material delivery, rolling-stock, labor transport |
| HSE Focus | Explosives, heavy vehicles, confined zones | Traffic control, pedestrian safety, night-work |

## Key Constraints by Domain

### Mining-Civil Constraints
- Client-specific safety standards (GAC/EGA-style)
- Strict environmental control plans
- High-precision logistics requirements
- Cycle-oriented sequential work phases

### Road-Civil Constraints
- National road design codes
- Traffic management requirements
- Pedestrian safety considerations
- Government/donor compliance requirements

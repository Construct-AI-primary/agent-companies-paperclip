1. Description of Variation:

This instruction is issued to direct the Contractor to construct foundation piles for the repaired
support structures (as generally indicated on drawing DWG-STR-FD-012 Rev B) to depths greater
than those indicated in the original tender documents and Bill of Quantities.

This variation is necessitated by findings from the detailed "Geotechnical investigations" (SoW
Clause 2.1.1), specifically report [GeoInvest-DHBEP-Final-June2023], which identified that
competent founding strata are located at a greater depth than initially anticipated to achieve the
required design bearing capacity for the repaired bridge structure.

2. Scope of Varied Work:

The varied work shall include, but not be limited to:

•Drilling/piling to the revised, increased depths as specified in DWG-STR-FD-012 Rev B
and any associated site instructions confirming specific pile termination depths.

•Provision and installation of additional steel reinforcement cages to suit the increased pile
lengths.

•Provision and placement of additional C40/50 concrete for the increased pile lengths.

•Any necessary adjustments to pile cap design if directly impacted by the increased pile
depth, subject to further review and instruction if applicable.

3. Impact on Cost:

The additional work resulting from this Variation Instruction shall be valued as follows:

•The additional volume of concrete and length of reinforcement directly attributable to the
increased pile depth (beyond the original BoQ estimated depths) will be measured on-site.

•Payment for the additional measured quantities of concrete and reinforcement will be
made at the existing BoQ rates for similar items, where applicable. If no directly applicable

rates exist, new rates shall be agreed upon between the Engineer and the Contractor in
accordance with the Contract.

•The Contractor is requested to submit a detailed claim for the costs associated with this
variation, including any preliminary and general costs directly attributable to the execution
of this varied work, for the Engineer's assessment.

4. Impact on Time:

Any impact on the Time for Completion resulting from this Variation Instruction will be assessed
by the Engineer upon submission of a detailed claim by the Contractor, in accordance with the
provisions of the Contract. The Contractor is to provide full particulars of any claim for an
extension of time.

5. Instruction:

The Contractor is hereby instructed to proceed with the works as described in this Variation
Instruction. All work is to be carried out in accordance with the Contract specifications, approved
drawings, and relevant quality standards.

Subject: Durban Highway Bridge Repair & Associated Works – Claim for Under-Measurement of
Concrete Works (IPC No. 5)

Dear Sir/Madam,

We are writing to formally register a concern regarding the measurement of concrete works
certified in Interim Payment Certificate No. 5, dated October 20, 2023, for the Durban Highway
Bridge Repair & Associated Works.

Our internal quantity assessments, cross-referenced with our concrete delivery dockets and pour
records for the period covered by IPC No. 5, indicate a significant discrepancy in the measured
quantities for structural concrete placed in the repaired foundations and support structures (as per
SoW Clause 2.2.4 "Bridge Widening") and the extended bridge deck elements (SoW Clause
2.2.4).

Specifically, our records show an additional 185 m³ of C40/50 concrete placed in these elements
beyond what has been certified. We believe this discrepancy arises from an under-measurement
of the actual in-situ volumes, potentially due to variations in foundation depths encountered and
design refinements to deck profiles which were executed as per site instructions and approved
drawing revisions (Ref: DWG-STR-BD-105 Rev C & DWG-STR-FD-012 Rev B).

We kindly request a joint site re-measurement and a review of the measurement records at your
earliest convenience to rectify this. We have attached our detailed reconciliation (Appendix A) for
your review.

We trust that this matter can be resolved amicably and expeditiously to ensure fair compensation
as per the contract terms and the "Payment Schedule" (SoW Clause 6.2).

Yours faithfully,

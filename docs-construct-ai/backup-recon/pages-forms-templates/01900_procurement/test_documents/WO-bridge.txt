Work Order Scope of Work: Durban Highway Bridge Repair & Associated Works
1. Work Order Overview
1.1 Background
This Work Order (WO) relates to specific works required on the existing concrete bridge spanning a 5-lane highway in Durban, South Africa. The broader highway upgrade project includes widening the carriageway to add two lanes. As part of this, defined portions of the bridge require repair, modification, or investigation — including works arising from disputed or non-conforming concrete.
1.2 Work Order Objectives
Depending on the assigned package, the objectives may include one or more of the following:
Repair and reinforce specified portions of the existing bridge structure
Undertake remedial works relating to disputed or defective concrete
Execute widening works for defined segments of the bridge
Minimize traffic disruption during all WO activities
Maintain full compliance with safety, quality, and environmental standards
Complete the WO tasks within the timeframe and deliverables defined by the Client
1.3 Site Location
The WO applies to the bridge located at Spaghetti Junction, Durban, South Africa. The area remains actively trafficked at all times.
1.4 Current Structure
The existing bridge consists of:
Concrete structure
50m span
100m length
40m widthThe defined area(s) for this Work Order will be confirmed by the Client before commencement.

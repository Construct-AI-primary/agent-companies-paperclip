Dear Sir/Madam,

We acknowledge receipt of your letter Ref: BB/DHBEP/C002 dated November 9, 2023, along with
Appendices B, C, and D, further to your initial claim (Ref: BB/DHBEP/C001) regarding concrete
quantities.

We have reviewed your submitted calculations and records. With respect to the increased pile
depths (your Appendix B and SoW 2.1.1 findings), we draw your attention to Variation
Instruction No. VI-003 (Deeper Foundation Piles), issued on [Insert Date, e.g., July 15, 2023].
This instruction acknowledged the necessity for increased pile depths based on the final
geotechnical data and approved design revision DWG-STR-FD-012 Rev B. VI-003 specifically
provides for the additional concrete volume directly attributable to the increased depth beyond
the original BoQ assumption, at agreed-upon rates for that specific varied work.

Therefore, any additional concrete volume related to the increased depth of piled foundations
only should be claimed under the provisions and remeasurement process outlined in VI-003.
Please ensure your claim for these specific volumes is submitted separately, or clearly itemized as
such, referencing VI-003, for our assessment under the terms of that Variation. The quantities
certified in IPC No. 5 for the standard BoQ item for foundations reflect the original design intent,
with the variance to be handled via VI-003.

Regarding your Appendix C (Deck Profile Analysis), we maintain that the minor adjustments to
deck thickness and camber, as per DWG-STR-BD-105 Rev C, represent design development
within the parameters of SoW 2.1.2 "Design and Engineering" and SoW 2.2.4 "Bridge Widening."
These are not considered to constitute a variation necessitating additional payment beyond the
BoQ rates unless a specific instruction altering the overall design envelope beyond reasonable
development was issued. Your "Detailed design drawings and calculations" (SoW Clause 5) were
indeed approved, but this approval does not automatically trigger additional payment for refined
quantities unless they fall outside the scope of the original BoQ items or are instructed as a Variation
under SoW Clause 6.3.

We are prepared to conduct a joint records review focusing on the application of VI-003 to the
foundation concrete. However, for the deck concrete, please provide further justification as to why

these design refinements should be considered a separately compensable change rather than a
standard evolution of the detailed design.

3. PRODUCT-SPECIFIC DELIVERY REQUIREMENTS

3.1 Engine Oils

Heavy Duty Diesel Engine Oil (LUB-ENG-HD-15W40): - Monthly Quantity: 12,000 liters - Packaging: 205L steel drums (approximately 58 drums per month) - Storage: Indoor storage required - Shelf Life: 5 years

Passenger Car Motor Oil (LUB-ENG-PC-5W30): - Monthly Quantity: 3,000 liters - Packaging: 5L plastic bottles (600 bottles per month) - Storage: Climate controlled storage - Shelf Life: 5 years

3.2 Hydraulic Oils

Anti-Wear Hydraulic Oil (LUB-HYD-AW-46): - Monthly Quantity: 30,000 liters - Packaging: 205L steel drums (approximately 146 drums per month) - Storage: Indoor storage, away from water - Shelf Life: 6 years

High Performance Hydraulic Oil (LUB-HYD-HP-68): - Monthly Quantity: 18,000 liters - Packaging: 205L steel drums (approximately 88 drums per month) - Storage: Indoor storage, temperature controlled - Shelf Life: 6 years

3.3 Gear Oils

Automotive Gear Oil (LUB-GEAR-AUTO-85W140): - Monthly Quantity: 12,000 liters - Packaging: 20L plastic pails (600 pails per month) - Storage: Indoor storage - Shelf Life: 5 years

Industrial Gear Oil (LUB-GEAR-IND-220): - Monthly Quantity: 7,000 liters - Packaging: 205L steel drums (approximately 34 drums per month) - Storage: Indoor storage - Shelf Life: 5 years

3.4 Greases

Multi-Purpose Grease (LUB-GREASE-MP-2): - Monthly Quantity: 10,000 kg - Packaging: 18kg pails (approximately 556 pails per month) - Storage: Indoor storage, cool and dry - Shelf Life: 3 years

High Temperature Grease (LUB-GREASE-HT-1): - Monthly Quantity: 5,000 kg - Packaging: 400g cartridges (12,500 cartridges per month) - Storage: Refrigerated storage - Shelf Life: 3 years

4. DELIVERY LOGISTICS

4.1 Transportation Requirements

Vehicle Specifications: - Clean, dedicated lubricant transport vehicles - Stainless steel or epoxy-lined tanks - Product segregation capabilities - Temperature controlled compartments

Driver Requirements: - Trained in hazardous materials handling - Valid dangerous goods license - Company uniform and identification - Emergency response training

4.2 Documentation Requirements

Delivery Note Requirements: - Purchase order number - Product codes and descriptions - Quantities delivered - Batch numbers and expiry dates - Certificate of Analysis reference - Safety Data Sheet reference

Quality Documentation: - Certificate of Analysis for each batch - Batch traceability records - Quality control test results - Shelf life certification

5. RECEIPT AND INSPECTION PROCEDURES

5.1 Delivery Acceptance

Visual Inspection: - Packaging integrity check - Label verification - Seal integrity confirmation - Damage assessment

Documentation Verification: - Delivery note accuracy - Certificate of Analysis presence - Batch number recording - Expiry date confirmation

5.2 Quality Control Sampling

Sampling Frequency: - One sample per product per delivery - Additional samples for large deliveries (>100 units)

Testing Requirements: - Visual inspection - Label verification - Basic property checks (if required) - Documentation completeness

6. STORAGE AND HANDLING REQUIREMENTS

6.1 Warehouse Requirements

Storage Conditions: - Temperature: 5°C to 40°C - Humidity: Maximum 80% RH - Clean and dry environment - Adequate ventilation

Storage Layout: - Product segregation by type - FIFO (First In, First Out) system - Accessibility for inspection - Spill containment provisions

6.2 Handling Equipment

Required Equipment: - Pallet jacks and forklifts - Drum handling equipment - Spill containment materials - PPE storage facilities

Safety Requirements: - Trained personnel only - Proper lifting techniques - Spill prevention measures - Emergency response equipment

7. DELIVERY PERFORMANCE MEASURES

7.1 On-Time Delivery

Target Performance: - 100% on-time deliveries - Delivery window compliance - Documentation completeness - Quality compliance

Measurement: - Delivery within scheduled window - Complete and accurate documentation - Product quality compliance - Customer satisfaction rating

7.2 Quality Metrics

Acceptance Criteria: - 100% documentation compliance - 100% packaging integrity - 100% label accuracy - 100% batch traceability

Non-Conformance Handling: - Immediate notification to supplier - Investigation and root cause analysis - Corrective action implementation - Preventive measures establishment

8. CONTINGENCY AND BACKUP PLANS

8.1 Delivery Disruptions

8.2 Emergency Deliveries

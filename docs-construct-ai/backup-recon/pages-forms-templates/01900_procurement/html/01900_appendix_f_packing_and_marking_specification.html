APPENDIX F: PACKING AND MARKING SPECIFICATION

Product/Material Supply - Packing and Marking Specification

1. GENERAL REQUIREMENTS

1.1 Scope

This appendix specifies the packing and marking requirements for all products/materials supplied under this Purchase Order. All packaging must ensure product integrity, safety, and compliance with transportation regulations.

1.2 Packaging Standards

All packaging must comply with: - International standards (ISO, ASTM, API) - Transportation regulations (ADR, IMDG, IATA) - Environmental standards (recycling, disposal) - Company packaging specifications - Customer handling requirements

2. PRIMARY PACKAGING REQUIREMENTS

2.1 Steel Drums (205L)

Technical Specifications: - Material: Cold-rolled steel, tin-plated interior - Capacity: 205 liters nominal - Wall thickness: 0.8mm minimum - Internal coating: Epoxy phenolic or equivalent - UN certification: Required for hazardous materials

Construction Requirements: - Seamless or welded construction - Pressure tested to 0.5 bar - Leak-proof design - Stackable up to 3 high when full - Forklift and pallet jack accessible

Quality Standards: - Clean and dry interior - No contamination from previous contents - Proper sealing with bungs and gaskets - Pressure relief devices where required

2.2 Plastic Bottles (1L, 4L, 5L)

Technical Specifications: - Material: High-density polyethylene (HDPE) - UV stabilization: Required for outdoor storage - Chemical resistance: Compatible with lubricant formulations - Wall thickness: 2mm minimum - Tamper-evident caps: Required

Design Requirements: - Ergonomic shape for handling - Wide neck for easy filling and dispensing - Graduated markings for measurement - Child-resistant closures for retail products - Recyclable material

2.3 Plastic Pails (20L, 25L)

Technical Specifications: - Material: HDPE with UV stabilization - Capacity: 20L or 25L nominal - Wall thickness: 3mm minimum - UN certification: Required for hazardous materials - Handle design: Reinforced plastic handles

Construction Features: - Blow-molded construction - Tamper-evident seals - Pouring spout with cap - Stackable design - Recyclable material

2.4 Grease Cartridges (400g)

Technical Specifications: - Material: Aluminum or composite construction - Capacity: 400g nominal - Thread type: Standard grease gun fitting - Seal integrity: Tamper-evident design - Shelf life: 3 years minimum

Design Requirements: - Easy loading into grease guns - Clean dispensing - No leakage during storage - Recyclable components

3. SECONDARY PACKAGING REQUIREMENTS

3.1 Pallet Configuration

Standard Pallet Specifications: - Type: EUR/EPAL pallet (1200mm x 800mm) - Load capacity: 1500kg minimum - Material: Heat-treated wood - ISPM 15 compliant: Required for international shipping

Pallet Load Configurations: - Steel drums: 4 drums per pallet (2x2 configuration) - Plastic pails: 36 x 20L pails per pallet - Plastic bottles: 48 x 5L bottles per pallet - Grease cartridges: 120 cartridges per pallet (6x20 configuration)

3.2 Stretch Wrapping

Requirements: - Material: LLDPE stretch film - Thickness: 20-25 micron - Pre-stretch ratio: 200-300% - Color: Clear or black - UV stabilization: Required

Wrapping Specifications: - Complete pallet coverage - 4-6 layers minimum - Secure base layer - Weather protection - Load stability

3.3 Protective Packaging

Corner Protectors: - Material: Cardboard or plastic - Thickness: 5mm minimum - Size: 50mm x 50mm x pallet height - Quantity: 4 per pallet

Top Covers: - Material: Corrugated cardboard or plastic sheet - Weather resistant: Required - Secure attachment: Stretch wrapped - Product identification: Visible through cover

4. MARKING AND LABELING REQUIREMENTS

4.1 Primary Container Labels

Required Information: - Product name and code - Net quantity and gross weight - Batch number and manufacturing date - Expiry date or shelf life - Supplier name and contact details - Hazard classification (if applicable) - Handling instructions

Label Specifications: - Material: Adhesive vinyl or polyester - Size: Minimum 100mm x 150mm - Color: White background with black text - Language: English primary, additional as required - Durability: Weather and chemical resistant

4.2 Secondary Packaging Labels

Pallet Labels: - Product description - Quantity per pallet - Gross weight and dimensions - Batch numbers - Handling instructions - Destination information

Label Specifications: - Size: Minimum 200mm x 300mm - Material: Weather-resistant adhesive - Barcode: GS1-128 format - Color coding: Product type identification

4.3 Hazard Labels and Placards

Dangerous Goods Labels: - Hazard class and division - UN number - Proper shipping name - Packing group - Emergency contact information

Regulatory Compliance: - GHS labeling requirements - Transportation regulations - Local regulatory requirements - Customer-specific requirements

5. PACKAGING TESTING AND QUALITY CONTROL

5.1 Packaging Integrity Testing

Drop Testing: - Height: 1.2m for individual containers - Surface: Concrete floor - Orientation: Most vulnerable position - Acceptance: No leakage or damage

Stack Testing: - Duration: 24 hours minimum - Load: 3 times pallet weight - Temperature: 40°C - Acceptance: No deformation or leakage

Vibration Testing: - Frequency: 5-200 Hz - Duration: 1 hour per axis - Amplitude: 0.5mm - Acceptance: No leakage or damage

5.2 Quality Control Procedures

Incoming Inspection: - Raw material verification - Manufacturing quality checks - Dimensional verification - Material compatibility testing

In-Process Inspection: - Filling accuracy verification - Seal integrity testing - Label application checks - Packaging completeness verification

Final Inspection: - Complete pallet inspection - Documentation verification - Sample retention - Release authorization

6. STORAGE AND HANDLING REQUIREMENTS

6.1 Packaging Storage Conditions

Temperature Requirements: - Drums and pails: 5°C to 40°C - Plastic bottles: 5°C to 35°C - Grease cartridges: 5°C to 25°C

Humidity Control: - Maximum 80% relative humidity - Protection from condensation - Dry storage areas

Light Protection: - UV protection required - Indoor storage preferred - Covered outdoor storage allowed with protection

6.2 Handling Procedures

Manual Handling: - Weight limits: 25kg maximum per person - Lifting equipment: Required for heavier loads - Team lifting: For loads over 50kg - Proper lifting techniques

Mechanical Handling: - Forklift certification required - Load stability verification - Speed limits in storage areas - Operator training requirements

7. ENVIRONMENTAL AND SUSTAINABILITY REQUIREMENTS

7.1 Recyclable Materials

Material Specifications: - Steel drums: 100% recyclable - Plastic containers: HDPE recyclable - Cardboard packaging: Recyclable - Pallets: Reusable or recyclable

Recycling Programs: - Supplier recycling responsibility - Customer return programs - Recycling certification - Environmental impact reporting

7.2 Minimized Packaging

Optimization Requirements: - Right-size packaging - Multi-purpose materials - Reduced material usage - Efficient space utilization

Waste Reduction: - Reusable packaging where possible - Returnable container programs - Waste minimization targets - Environmental impact assessment

8. PACKAGING DOCUMENTATION

8.1 Packaging Specifications

Technical Documentation: - Detailed drawings and specifications - Material certificates - Test certificates - Quality control records

Change Control: - Packaging change procedures - Approval requirements - Customer notification - Documentation updates

8.2 Traceability Records

Batch Documentation: - Packaging batch numbers - Manufacturing dates - Quality test results - Supplier certifications

Traceability System: - Unique identification codes - Batch tracking capabilities - Recall procedures - Audit trail maintenance

9. TRANSPORTATION PACKAGING

9.1 Unit Load Configuration

Pallet Specifications: - Standard dimensions: 1200mm x 800mm - Maximum height: 1800mm - Weight distribution: Even loading - Stability requirements: No shifting during transport

Load Securing: - Stretch wrap requirements - Strapping specifications - Blocking and bracing - Load stability testing

9.2 Container Loading

Container Specifications: - 20ft container: 33 Europallets - 40ft container: 66 Europallets - Load distribution: Weight and height balanced - Ventilation requirements: As needed

Loading Procedures: - Weight verification - Securement checks - Documentation attachment - Seal application

10. PACKAGING PERFORMANCE MONITORING

10.1 Damage Reporting

Incident Documentation: - Damage description and location - Packaging condition assessment - Contributing factors analysis - Corrective action recommendations

Reporting Procedures: - Immediate notification requirements - Photographic documentation - Investigation procedures - Follow-up actions

10.2 Performance Metrics

Key Performance Indicators: - Packaging damage rate (<1%) - Customer satisfaction scores - On-time delivery performance - Cost efficiency metrics

Continuous Improvement: - Regular performance reviews - Packaging optimization projects - Supplier performance monitoring - Industry benchmarking

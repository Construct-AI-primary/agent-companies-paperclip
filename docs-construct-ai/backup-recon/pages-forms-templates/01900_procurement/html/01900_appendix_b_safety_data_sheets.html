APPENDIX B: SAFETY DATA SHEETS (SDS)

Product/Material Supply - Safety Data Sheets

1. GENERAL REQUIREMENTS

1.1 Scope

This appendix contains Safety Data Sheets (SDS) for all products/materials supplied under this Purchase Order. SDS are provided in accordance with: - GHS (Globally Harmonized System) for classification and labeling - OSHA Hazard Communication Standard 29 CFR 1910.1200 - REACH Regulation (EC) No 1907/2006 - CLP Regulation (EC) No 1272/2008

1.2 SDS Requirements

Each SDS must contain 16 sections as per GHS requirements: 1. Identification 2. Hazard identification 3. Composition/information on ingredients 4. First-aid measures 5. Fire-fighting measures 6. Accidental release measures 7. Handling and storage 8. Exposure controls/personal protection 9. Physical and chemical properties 10. Stability and reactivity 11. Toxicological information 12. Ecological information 13. Disposal considerations 14. Transport information 15. Regulatory information 16. Other information

5. HAZARD COMMUNICATION

5.1 Label Requirements

All lubricant containers must be labeled with: - Product name and code - Supplier contact information - Hazard classification (if applicable) - Safety precautions - Batch number and date

5.2 Training Requirements

Personnel handling lubricants must be trained in: - Safe handling procedures - Spill response - PPE usage - Emergency procedures - Waste disposal

6. EMERGENCY RESPONSE

6.1 Spill Response

Small Spills (<25L): - Contain spill with absorbent material - Clean up with appropriate absorbents - Dispose as hazardous waste if required

Large Spills (>25L): - Evacuate area - Contact emergency services - Contain and prevent spread - Professional cleanup required

6.2 Fire Response

  • Use appropriate extinguishing media
  • Wear self-contained breathing apparatus
  • Cool containers to prevent rupture
  • Prevent runoff into drains

6.3 Medical Emergency

  • Contact poison control center
  • Provide SDS to medical personnel
  • Transport to nearest medical facility
  • Report incident to supplier

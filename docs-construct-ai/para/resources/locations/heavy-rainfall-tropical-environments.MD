# Heavy Rainfall & Tropical Environments

## Environmental Conditions
- **Heavy rainfall**: Monsoon seasons with 2000-10000mm annual rainfall, intense downpours causing flash flooding
- **High humidity**: 80-100% relative humidity accelerating corrosion and mold/biological growth
- **Flooding**: Seasonal flooding, washed-out access roads, submerged works
- **Tropical diseases**: Malaria, dengue, cholera, and other vector-borne and waterborne diseases

## Discipline-Specific Impacts
- **Civil/Structural**: Earthworks instability in wet conditions, soil erosion, rapid water ingress into excavations, concrete washout
- **Mechanical**: Accelerated corrosion in humid environments, mold in enclosed spaces, hydraulic fluid contamination
- **Electrical**: Condensation inside enclosures causing ground faults, high lightning strike frequency, moisture damage to sensitive equipment
- **HSE**: Tropical disease transmission, heat-humidity stress, flooded and unstable work areas, vector-borne diseases, waterborne illnesses

## Procurement Considerations
- **PO**: Corrosion-resistant materials (316L stainless, aluminum, marine-grade), humidity-controlled enclosures, tropical-disease PPE (mosquito nets, repellents), water treatment equipment
- **WO**: Dewatering services, erosion control works, drainage construction, disease vector control services, water treatment
- **SO**: Verify tropical-grade specifications, IP ratings, humidity resistance, anti-mold treatments, insect-resistant packaging

## Health & Safety
- Malaria and dengue prophylaxis programs, vaccination programs
- Active elimination of disease-carrying water breeding grounds
- Wet bulb globe temperature (WBGT) monitoring for heat-humidity stress
- Flood evacuation plans, potable water provision, food safety protocols

## Logistics & Mobilization
- Pre-monsoon material stockpiling for wet season continuity
- Elevated, covered laydown areas with comprehensive drainage systems
- All-weather access roads with crown, side drains, and culverts
- Emergency supply chains for flood-related disruptions

## Mitigation Measures
- Seasonal scheduling: major earthworks and concrete pours in dry season
- Comprehensive site drainage and water management systems from day one
- Humidity-controlled storage for electrical and sensitive equipment
- Regular vector control programs (fumigation, larviciding)
- Monsoon contingency planning with schedule float and alternative sequencing
- Concrete protection from rapid washout using covers and proper curing

# IntegrateForge AI Team Documentation

## Overview

IntegrateForge AI is an autonomous AI company specializing in third-party tool integrations for the engineering and construction ecosystem. The company provides seamless connectivity between Construct-AI and external engineering software platforms, enabling users to work with their preferred tools while benefiting from AI-powered workflows.

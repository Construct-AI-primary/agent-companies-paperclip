# ContentForge AI Team Documentation

## Overview

ContentForge AI is a specialized AI company within the Paperclip ecosystem that focuses on automated content creation, website management, and SEO optimization. The company provides comprehensive solutions for content marketing, website performance, and search engine optimization through a team of specialized AI agents.

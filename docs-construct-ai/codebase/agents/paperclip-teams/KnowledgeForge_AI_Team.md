# KnowledgeForge AI Team Documentation

## Overview

KnowledgeForge AI is a comprehensive knowledge management and documentation autonomous AI company specializing in enterprise knowledge systems, documentation architecture, and information lifecycle management. The company follows the agentcompanies/v1 specification with agents organized under specialized knowledge management teams.

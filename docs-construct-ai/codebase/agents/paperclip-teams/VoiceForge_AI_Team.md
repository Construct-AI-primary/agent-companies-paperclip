## Key Capabilities

### Speech Processing & Recognition
- High-accuracy speech-to-text (50+ languages)
- Real-time, low-latency processing
- Noise cancellation and echo reduction
- Accent adaptation and speaker recognition

### Voice Synthesis & Audio
- Natural text-to-speech with emotional expression
- Voice cloning and personalization
- Multilingual synthesis with native pronunciation
- Audio enhancement and spatial audio

### Multimodal Integration
- Voice + visual integration
- Gesture and motion recognition
- Emotion detection and response adaptation
- Cross-modal fusion

## Technology Stack

- **Speech Models**: Custom transformer-based STT/TTS
- **Language Models**: Large language models for NLU
- **Infrastructure**: Edge computing, GPU acceleration, global CDN
- **APIs**: REST/GraphQL, WebRTC, WebSockets

## Performance Metrics

- **Word Error Rate**: <3% across supported languages
- **Real-time Latency**: <300ms end-to-end
- **Intent Recognition**: >95% accuracy

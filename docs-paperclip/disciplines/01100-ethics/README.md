# 01100 — Ethics

The Ethics discipline covers business ethics, corporate governance, compliance, integrity management, and ethical decision-making frameworks for construction and engineering organizations.

## Overview

This discipline ensures ethical conduct across all operations, maintains integrity standards, implements anti-corruption measures, and fosters a culture of transparency and accountability. It encompasses Code of Conduct management, Anti-Bribery & Anti-Corruption (ABAC) compliance, Whistleblower management, Ethics Training & Awareness, Conflicts of Interest management, and Gifts/Hospitality/Donations governance.

## Key Responsibilities

- Code of Conduct development, dissemination, and acknowledgment tracking
- Anti-Bribery & Anti-Corruption compliance (FCPA, UK Bribery Act, ISO 37001)
- Whistleblower management and protection (8-step process)
- Ethics training and awareness programs
- Conflicts of Interest identification and management
- Gifts, hospitality, and donations policy enforcement
- Ethics compliance monitoring and reporting
- Ethical decision-making frameworks and guidance

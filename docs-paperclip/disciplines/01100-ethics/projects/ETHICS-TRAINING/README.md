# ETHICS-TRAINING — Ethics Training & Awareness

Build a comprehensive ethics training and awareness platform with curriculum management, course assignment, completion tracking, certification, and compliance gap analysis for construction and engineering organizations.

## Platforms

This project supports **desktop**, **mobile**, and **web** platforms.

### Desktop Platform
- **Target Users:** Training administrators, ethics officers, compliance managers
- **Key Features:** Curriculum builder, course assignment, completion tracking, certification management
- **UI/UX Focus:** SaaSForge AI

### Mobile Platform
- **Target Users:** Employees, field personnel, managers
- **Key Features:** Course access, offline learning, quiz completion, certificate download
- **UI/UX Focus:** SaaSForge AI

### Web Platform
- **Target Users:** Employees, managers, training administrators
- **Key Features:** Course catalog, enrollment, completion tracking, training history
- **UI/UX Focus:** SaaSForge AI

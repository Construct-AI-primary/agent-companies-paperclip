# ETHICS-ABAC Implementation Plan

Status: **Planning Phase** 📋
Owner: Ethics Discipline Team
Date: 2026-05-04
Last Updated: 2026-05-04

## Summary

Implement the **ETHICS-ABAC Anti-Bribery & Anti-Corruption Compliance System** for the 01100 ethics discipline. This project delivers a comprehensive ABAC platform covering FCPA/UK Bribery Act/ISO 37001 compliance, risk assessment, third-party due diligence, gift and hospitality register, political contributions tracking, and compliance monitoring.

**Core Capabilities:**
1. **ABAC Framework** — Regulatory compliance framework, risk assessment model, policy templates
2. **ABAC Core** — Gift/hospitality register, third-party due diligence, risk assessment engine
3. **Integration** — Cross-discipline compliance alignment, training integration, whistleblower linkage
4. **Mobile** — Mobile gift declaration, due diligence requests, policy access
5. **Compliance** — ABAC audit trail, compliance reporting, regulatory filing

**Total Implementation:** 5 phases × multiple tasks per phase

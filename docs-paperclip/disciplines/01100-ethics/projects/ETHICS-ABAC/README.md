# ETHICS-ABAC — Anti-Bribery & Anti-Corruption

Build a comprehensive Anti-Bribery & Anti-Corruption (ABAC) compliance platform covering FCPA, UK Bribery Act, and ISO 37001 compliance with risk assessment, third-party due diligence, gift register, and compliance monitoring for construction and engineering organizations.

## Platforms

This project supports **desktop**, **mobile**, and **web** platforms.

### Desktop Platform
- **Target Users:** Compliance officers, ethics managers, internal audit
- **Key Features:** ABAC risk assessment, third-party due diligence, gift register, compliance monitoring
- **UI/UX Focus:** SaaSForge AI

### Mobile Platform
- **Target Users:** Field personnel, managers, employees
- **Key Features:** Gift declaration, compliance training, policy access, risk reporting
- **UI/UX Focus:** SaaSForge AI

### Web Platform
- **Target Users:** Employees, managers, third parties
- **Key Features:** Gift declaration, due diligence requests, compliance dashboard, training portal
- **UI/UX Focus:** SaaSForge AI

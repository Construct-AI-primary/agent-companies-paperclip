# 01600 - Local Content

The Local Content discipline covers local employment, local procurement, enterprise development, skills transfer, and compliance reporting for construction and engineering projects.

## Overview

This discipline ensures compliance with local content regulations, promotes local economic development through employment and procurement, supports enterprise and supplier development, implements skills transfer programs, and maintains comprehensive compliance reporting aligned with IFC PS1 standards.

## Key Responsibilities

- Local employment and workforce localisation management
- Local procurement and supply chain development
- Enterprise development and supplier development programs
- Skills transfer and training development
- Local content compliance reporting
- Stakeholder engagement with local communities
- Socio-economic impact monitoring
- Local content performance tracking

## Integration

- Works with Procurement for local sourcing and supply chain
- Coordinates with Finance for benefit tracking and local spend
- Partners with Projects for site integration and workforce planning
- Interfaces with Legal for compliance and regulatory requirements
- Aligns with HSE for community health and safety
- Reports to Governance for local content performance

- IFC PS1 (Performance Standard 1) вЂ” Assessment and Management of Environmental and Social Risks
- Local content regulatory requirements
- Employment equity and localisation targets
- Enterprise development scorecards
- Skills transfer verification frameworks
- Local procurement spend reporting

## Overview

The HEALTH-SURVEILLANCE project delivers a comprehensive Medical Surveillance & Occupational Health Monitoring system. It encompasses surveillance scheduling, test result management, health records, exposure tracking, fitness-for-work assessments, and regulatory compliance for occupational health programs.

## Surveillance Programs

- Audiometry (hearing conservation)
- Spirometry (respiratory surveillance)
- Biological monitoring (blood, urine)
- Health screening (general medicals)
- Dermatological surveillance
- HAVS (hand-arm vibration syndrome)
- Respiratory fit testing

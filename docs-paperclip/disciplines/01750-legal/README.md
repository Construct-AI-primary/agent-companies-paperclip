# 01750 - Legal

The Legal discipline covers contract law, claims & dispute resolution, regulatory compliance, legal documentation, and corporate governance for construction and engineering organizations.

## Overview

This discipline ensures legal compliance, protects organizational interests, manages legal risks, and provides legal support across all operations and projects. It encompasses contract management (FIDIC, NEC, JCT), claims and dispute resolution, regulatory compliance (labour, environmental, H&S, anti-bribery, data protection), legal documentation, and corporate governance.

## Key Responsibilities

- Contract law and administration (FIDIC, NEC, JCT frameworks)
- Claims management and dispute resolution
- Regulatory compliance monitoring and reporting
- Legal documentation and record management
- Corporate governance and board support
- Legal risk assessment and mitigation
- Intellectual property protection
- Regulatory liaison and engagement

## Integration

- Works with Contracts Director for contract legal review and FIDIC/NEC/JCT compliance
- Coordinates with Governance for corporate governance and board support
- Partners with Projects for legal support and claims management
- Interfaces with Procurement for vendor agreements and contract negotiation
- Collaborates with HSE for regulatory compliance (labour, environmental, H&S)
- Aligns with Finance for anti-bribery compliance and financial governance
- Coordinates with IT for data protection and cybersecurity legal requirements

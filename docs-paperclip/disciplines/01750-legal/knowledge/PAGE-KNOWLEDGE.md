# 01750 Legal - Knowledge Base

## Key Legal References

### Contract Law Frameworks

| Framework | Description | Key Applications |
|-----------|-------------|------------------|
| **FIDIC** | International Federation of Consulting Engineers - Standard contracts for construction | Red Book (Building/Engineering), Yellow Book (Plant/Design-Build), Silver Book (EPC/Turnkey) |
| **NEC** | New Engineering Contract - Flexible, collaborative contracts | NEC4 suite: ECC (Engineering & Construction), PSC (Professional Services), TSC (Term Service) |
| **JCT** | Joint Contracts Tribunal - UK standard construction contracts | Design & Build, Standard Building Contract, Minor Works, Management Contracting |

### Claims & Dispute Resolution - 6-Step Process

1. **Notification** — Timely notice of claim event within contractually specified period
2. **Documentation** — Gather contemporaneous records, site diaries, photographs, correspondence
3. **Evaluation** — Assess entitlement under contract, quantify delay and/or cost impact
4. **Submission** — Prepare and submit formal claim with supporting evidence
5. **Negotiation** — Engage in without-prejudice discussions, mediation, or adjudication
6. **Resolution** — Final determination via adjudication, arbitration, or litigation

| Area | Key Regulations | Compliance Requirements |
|------|----------------|------------------------|
| **Labour** | Employment laws, labour standards, working time regulations | Contracts, wages, working hours, termination procedures |
| **Environmental** | Environmental protection acts, waste management, emissions | Permits, impact assessments, remediation plans |
| **Health & Safety** | H&S legislation, construction regulations | Risk assessments, safety plans, incident reporting |
| **Anti-Bribery** | Bribery acts, anti-corruption laws | Policies, training, due diligence, gift registers |

### Legal Documentation Types

- **Contracts** — Construction contracts, service agreements, NDAs, MOUs
- **Claims** — Notice of claims, quantum submissions, extension of time claims
- **Compliance** — Regulatory filings, permits, licences, compliance reports
- **Governance** — Board minutes, resolutions, policies, registers
- **Correspondence** — Legal letters, without-prejudice communications, formal notices

## Key Terminology

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Force Majeure** | Unforeseeable circumstances preventing contract performance |
| **Liquidated Damages** | Pre-estimated damages for delay or non-performance |
| **Variation** | Change to scope, cost, or schedule under contract |
| **Adjudication** | Statutory dispute resolution process (UK Housing Grants Act) |
| **Arbitration** | Private dispute resolution by appointed arbitrator(s) |
| **Mediation** | Without-prejudice facilitated negotiation |
| **Lien** | Legal right to retain property until debt is paid |
| **Indemnity** | Protection against loss or liability |
| **Novation** | Transfer of contractual rights and obligations |
| **Subrogation** | Substitution of one party for another in legal rights |

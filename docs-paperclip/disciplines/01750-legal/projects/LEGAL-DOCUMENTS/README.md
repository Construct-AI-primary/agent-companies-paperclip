# LEGAL-DOCUMENTS — Legal Document Management & Governance

Build a comprehensive legal document management and corporate governance platform covering document repository, version control, approval workflows, retention policies, and governance record management for construction and engineering organizations.

## Platforms

This project supports **desktop**, **mobile**, and **web** platforms.

### Desktop Platform
- **Target Users:** Document controllers, legal counsel, governance officers
- **Key Features:** Document repository, version control, approval workflows, retention management, governance records

### Mobile Platform
- **Target Users:** Legal counsel, document reviewers, governance officers
- **Key Features:** Mobile document viewing, annotation, approval actions, offline access

### Web Platform
- **Target Users:** Document controllers, legal directors, auditors
- **Key Features:** Document dashboard, search, governance records, reporting

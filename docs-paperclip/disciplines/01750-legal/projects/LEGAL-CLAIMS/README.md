Build a comprehensive claims and dispute resolution platform covering the full 6-step claims process (Notification, Documentation, Evaluation, Submission, Negotiation, Resolution) for construction and engineering projects.

## Platforms

This project supports **desktop**, **mobile**, and **web** platforms.

### Desktop Platform
- **Target Users:** Claims managers, legal counsel, dispute resolution specialists
- **Key Features:** Claims intake, 6-step process tracking, quantum analysis, dispute case management, adjudication/arbitration support

### Mobile Platform
- **Target Users:** Claims managers, site personnel, legal counsel
- **Key Features:** Mobile claims intake, photo/document attachment, claim status tracking, push notifications

### Web Platform
- **Target Users:** Claims managers, legal directors, auditors
- **Key Features:** Claims dashboard, case management, reporting and analytics

# LEGAL-COMPLIANCE — Regulatory Compliance & Legal Risk

Build a comprehensive regulatory compliance and legal risk management platform covering labour, environmental, health & safety, anti-bribery, and data protection compliance for construction and engineering projects.

## Platforms

This project supports **desktop**, **mobile**, and **web** platforms.

### Desktop Platform
- **Target Users:** Compliance officers, legal counsel, risk managers
- **Key Features:** Compliance obligation register, regulatory monitoring, risk assessment, corrective action tracking, compliance reporting

### Mobile Platform
- **Target Users:** Compliance officers, site managers, legal counsel
- **Key Features:** Mobile compliance checks, alert acknowledgment, compliance calendar, offline access

### Web Platform
- **Target Users:** Compliance officers, legal directors, auditors
- **Key Features:** Compliance dashboard, obligation management, reporting and analytics

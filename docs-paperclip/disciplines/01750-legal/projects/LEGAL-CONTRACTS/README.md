# LEGAL-CONTRACTS — Contract Management & Administration

Build a comprehensive contract management and administration platform covering contract creation, lifecycle management, obligation tracking, approval routing, and compliance monitoring for construction contracts under FIDIC, NEC, and JCT frameworks.

## Platforms

This project supports **desktop**, **mobile**, and **web** platforms to ensure comprehensive coverage of contract management across all operational contexts.

### Desktop Platform
- **Target Users:** Legal counsel, contract managers, compliance officers
- **Key Features:** Contract creation with FIDIC/NEC/JCT templates, clause library, obligation tracking, approval routing, compliance monitoring
- **UI/UX Focus:** SaaSForge AI

### Mobile Platform
- **Target Users:** Legal counsel, contract reviewers, field managers
- **Key Features:** Mobile contract review, approval actions, document viewing, offline access
- **UI/UX Focus:** SaaSForge AI

### Web Platform
- **Target Users:** Contract managers, compliance officers, auditors
- **Key Features:** Contract dashboard, admin interface, audit trail viewer
- **UI/UX Focus:** SaaSForge AI
